What is Spectra?

Spectra is a decentralized network that allows for blazing fasting hosting, storage, and microservices. Spectra works by allowing anyone to sell their compute power. This allows Spectra users to utilize a wide variety of hardware, and pay for only what they need.

Products:

Spectra Hosting



Host a website on the Spectra network, and pay only when people visit your site.

Spectra Node



Donwload and Run The Spectra Node app for desktop, and make money contributing to the Spectra network.
